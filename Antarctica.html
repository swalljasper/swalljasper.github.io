Terrain Map of Antarctica

I designed this map of Antarctica for On Wisconsin Magazine, showing the locations of UW grads who are there on research. It incorporates a blender shaded relief, point data, and bathemtry smoothed in Photoshop. I experimented with adding in blue overlays in the shadows and yellow in the highlights in the swiss style to give it some intricacies across the vast whiteness. The print scale of this map is 11x17in.
