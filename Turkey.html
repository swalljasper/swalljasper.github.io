Historic Maps of Anatolia

These maps are a sample of an in progress project for a book on Hellenistic Anatolia. They are a series of shaded relief maps at various scales that have complex data overlaying intense terrain. The idea is to show how the places fit into the natural area surrounding them. I made them using ArcGIS, Blender, Photoshop and Illustrator. The data isn't ready for the second map, however the basemap shows how the same terrain can be expressed in different ways. Note: These maps are still works in progress, so please excuse any copy errors.
