What Do I Do?

I am Soren Walljasper, Cartographer, Geographer, and Designer. I am currently an undergrad at UW-Madison studying Human Geography, Cartography/GIS, and Environmental Studies.

I'm a Sailor, Skier, Biker, and Climber. Most of all I love the outdoors, both natural and urban. Find out about my experience here

If you have a good cause that is in need of some visual expression, I can help. I am a believer that neither form nor function follow one another: they cannot exist without each other. Contact me if you need a map or graphic.

All graphics and textures on this site are original works by me
