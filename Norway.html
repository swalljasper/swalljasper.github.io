Mapping a Saga

This is one of two maps I made for a literature class that I took on Icelandic Sagas. It shows the locations of key events and characters in the story. The challenge in making this map was matching the graphic styles of the map with the nature of the saga. To see more of my stylistic work with textures, see my map of Lake Superior
